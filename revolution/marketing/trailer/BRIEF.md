## Intent

Ship a concise atmospheric trailer now from the strongest material already in
the build. The piece should feel like an interactive documentary, not a game
trailer: restrained, historically earnest, physical, and cinematic.

## Assets

- `../public/assets/worlds/` — existing rendered historical environments.
- `../public/reference/` — existing conditioning and reference imagery.
- `../artifacts/music-theme-candidates/takes/` — existing 36-second period-instrument music candidates.
- Live local build — title screen and usable Tea Party, Lexington, Delaware,
  Valley Forge, and Treaty of Paris views.

## Customizations

- Full-frame environment imagery with restrained parallax and camera drift.
- Ink-and-paper title cards using the application's exact palette.
- Primary copy: “Walk the frozen moments. History moves with you. Sign your name to history.”
- Second-pass framing distinguishes World Labs exploration from Reactor's high-action generative participation.
- Low diegetic ambience from each historical environment sits beneath the period-instrument score.
- The close invites the viewer to enter the story and leave a mark.
- Deliver an editable HyperFrames project and a 1920×1080 MP4.

## Notes

- Use only existing material; do not generate new images, voiceover, music, or footage.
- Do not show development controls, placeholders, loading states, or unfinished interactions.
- No close violence, gore, triumphal military spectacle, modern trailer booms, or glitch effects.
- Do not reveal the finale's aged-signature payoff.
