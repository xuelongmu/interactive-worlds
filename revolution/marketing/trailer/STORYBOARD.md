## Video direction

Use the `frame.md` palette verbatim: near-black paper, white ink, parchment sun,
and restrained gold ember. Environment plates are full-bleed and oversized so
the captured review controls and documentary subtitle band sit outside the final
frame. Georgia carries all display copy; JetBrains Mono carries dates and small
metadata. Motion is restrained and seek-safe: one deliberate camera move per
environment plate, long-tail `power3` settles, sequential type reveals, and
still final holds. No bounce, breathing cards, glitch, modern trailer graphics,
fake browser chrome, or decorative imagery unrelated to ink, paper, dates, and
hairline rules. Rhythm: drift → statement → tension → peak → pause → winter
breather → reflection → identity. The bottom caption band remains clear even
though narration captions are disabled.
